About Me

My full name is Gregory William Joseph D. Liu but people just call me Gram. I'm currently a rising sophomore at Carnegie Mellon University pursuing a major in Electrical and Computer Engineering.

On campus, my heart is in the work. I'm the Director of Technology for ScottyLabs, leading the Tech Committee that develops APIs and applications for the CMU community. I also do research at the CMU CHIMPS Lab, creating an IoT app development framework that enables the creation of secure smart home systems.

Off campus, I work with different initiatives to develop technology for social good. Over the summer, I worked with Dashboard Philippines, to centralize information and relief distribution in the Philippines for the COVID-19 pandemic. Check out my resume or my LinkedIn profile for more info.
